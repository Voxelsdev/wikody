1) Waiting

Please wait for the articles to load in before playing. This is indicated by the progress bar on top of the game's page.

2) Picking

Choose your category and point value. Categories are designated by column.

3) Guessing

Most articles have an underlined space which indicates the article title. Wikipedia is not perfect, not all articles are formatted the same, so if there is no underlined space, try to guess what the name of the article would be.

4) Scoring

The player who guesses the title of the wikipedia page correctly is awarded the proper amount of points, and it becomes the next player's turn.

5) Forfeiting

If the article is too hard, but you really need to know in what book did the Vogons destroyed the Earth, there is an option indicated by the !? icon to give up the points and get the answer.
